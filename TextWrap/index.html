Wrap

The feature does a little more than just ensure paragraphs don't end with a single word, it also adjusts hyphenation if consecutive hyphenated lines appear at the end of a paragraph or adjusts previous lines to make room. It will also appropriately adjust for text justification. text-wrap: pretty is for generally better line wrapping and text breaking, currently focused on orphans.
